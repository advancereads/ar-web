Frequently Asked

What's an ARC?

ARC stands for Advance Reader Copy. It's a pre-release version of a book, sent to readers before the official launch date. ARCs help authors gather early reviews, find their first readers, and build momentum for launch day. When you sign up with AdvanceReads, you're joining a community of readers who care about quality fiction and aren't afraid to be honest about what they read.

What do you expect from ARC readers?

An honest review on Amazon within 2 weeks of the book's official launch date. That's it. We're not looking for perfect reviews or five-star puff pieces — we're looking for genuine reactions. If you loved it, say so. If parts didn't land, say that too. Honest reviews (good or critical) are what help real readers decide whether a book is right for them.

What genres do you publish?

We're starting with thrillers, mysteries, and suspense novels. That's where our first authors and series live. More genres will come as we grow. When you sign up, tell us what you care about — we'll only send you ARCs that match your interests.

Is it really free?

Yes. No hidden costs. No surprise fees. No strings beyond the review. We believe the best marketing is a good book in the hands of a reader who'll be honest about it. That's the deal.

How often will I hear from you?

Only when there's something worth saying. When a new ARC is available, we'll email you. When a book launches, we'll let you know. We won't spam you with marketing noise or daily newsletters. You can also unsubscribe at any time — no judgment.

What if I don't finish the book?

Life happens. If you can't finish a book before launch, just let us know. You won't be blacklisted or charged. We'd rather you be honest than force a review you don't feel confident writing. That said, we do ask that you try — that's the commitment when you request an ARC.
